What SyncReady Does

Five layers of
sync intelligence
no one else provides.

Your music is already on SoundCloud, Spotify, and your publisher's site. What it doesn't have is the intelligence layer that makes a supervisor stop, listen, and license.

🎬
Scene Pitch Generator
Claude AI reads your sonic description, mood, and energy data and writes 3 specific scene pitches — the way an experienced music supervisor would describe your track to a director.
"Ideal for a slow-burn courtroom drama where the verdict is uncertain — the restraint does more damage than any dramatic swell would."
🔍
Emotional Keyword Mapping
Beyond "cinematic" and "tense" — Claude generates 10 emotional scene keywords supervisors actually search. The words that match the feeling of a moment, not just a genre.
"quiet devastation · last chance · surveillance footage · unlikely triumph · 3am decision"
📊
Editability Score
Waveform analysis grades your track on 7 real supervisor criteria: intro length, 30-second energy peak, breakdown presence, stinger ending, dynamic range, edit points, and instrumental availability.
"Score: 92/100 — lose 2 seconds off your intro and upload an instrumental to hit 100."
🏷️
One-Stop Certification
AI-guided Chain of Title screening — 3 targeted questions that catch uncleared samples, co-writer conflicts, and label encumbrances before they kill a deal. Badge reads "Artist-Declared · AI-Reviewed."
"Supervisors can license in a single call — no clearance risk, no back-and-forth."
🗺️
Catalog Gap Analysis
Pro and Catalog members get monthly AI analysis of their catalog's blind spots. What tempos are missing. What emotional tones are underrepresented. What supervisors in your genre are requesting that you're not making.
"Your catalog lacks high-energy tracks under 2:30 — the most requested length for trailer spots."
🔒
Metadata Locked Into File
Your contact info, scene pitches, Sync Score, and One-Stop status are burned directly into the WAV file. No matter where it travels — email, Slack, shared drives — your info goes with it forever.
"Downloaded 47 times. Your name and email are in every copy."
